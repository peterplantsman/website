Landscape Garden Design

Turning your garden space into a paradise

I am Peter the Plantsman

I have been an amateur gardener for over 40 years, and now put my knowledge and experience at the disposal of others who wish to work wonders in their own gardens.

I have supplemented my own knowledge with recognised qualifications from the Royal Horticultural Society, and offer professional garden development advice to private gardeners.

My aim is to provide practical advice, garden design and planting schemes at an affordable price.

I believe every garden can be a pleasure to its owner, regardless of their budget, available time or gardening knowledge.

I’m happy to help to create gardens, but even happier to help others to be the creators of their own unique gardens.

Hopefully I can be of some service to you.

5 Great Reasons to Create a New Garden

  1. It pays- It is estimated that a well-designed garden can add between 5% and 15% to the value of your property, so it can be a very worthwhile investment.
  2. It's good for you– Time spent in the open air is good for all of us, and if you do the gardening yourself it’s great exercise. Gardens can be beautiful places to escape into to relax and unwind too.
  3. It's productive- Your garden can be a source of a wide range of fresh fruit and vegetables, as well as flowers for the house. Home grown produce is nutritious, delicious and (almost) free.
  4. It's environmentally friendly– With a little care you can transform your garden into a haven for wildlife, from birds, bees and butterflies to hedgehogs & frogs.
  5. It's fun– A well designed garden can literally become an extra living space, perfect for entertaining and al fresco dining. It can also give you great personal pleasure and satisfaction.
