5 Great Reasons to Create a New Garden

It pays- It is estimated that a well-designed garden can add between 5% and 15% to the value of your property, so it can be a very worthwhile investment.

It's good for you– Time spent in the open air is good for all of us, and if you do the gardening yourself it’s great exercise. Gardens can be beautiful places to escape into to relax and unwind too.

It's productive- Your garden can be a source of a wide range of fresh fruit and vegetables, as well as flowers for the house. Home grown produce is nutritious, delicious and (almost) free.

It's environmentally friendly– With a little care you can transform your garden into a haven for wildlife, from birds, bees and butterflies to hedgehogs & frogs.

It's fun– A well designed garden can literally become an extra living space, perfect for entertaining and al fresco dining. It can also give you great personal pleasure and satisfaction.

Why a Plantsman?

I believe that it is plants that make a garden, and a great garden is made by combining the right plants in the right way to create a harmonious whole.

This takes knowledge & skill, and the experience of having worked with plants over a long period of time – it takes a plantsman.

A garden design may incorporate features other than plants of course – decking, ponds, paths and so on, but these are the domain of the builder.

A plantsman is not a builder, nor a builder a plantsman.

A complete garden scheme will combine the hard landscaping skills of the builder with the soft landscaping skills of the plantsman.
